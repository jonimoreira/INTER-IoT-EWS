Class LogiCO:Forwarder

rdf:type

rdfs:comment
Who is handling/organizing the shipment on behalf of somebody else that owns the goods. This organization sometimes is hust about having a phone and a desk, maybe a computer, and booking resources. In other cases, for example DHL, the forwarder also executes some parts because he owns resources (airplanes, trucks), but the forwarder never own the goods himself.
