dcterms:description
LogiServ (Logistics Service Ontology) models business activities (such as Transport, Transshipment, Load, Discharge, Storage, Consolidation, Deconsolidation, etc.) and their properties as the basis for specifying logistic services. LogiServ is an extension of LogiCO (Logistics Core Ontology).
